HillRunner Tower Defense

For centuries, the Sacred Hill has stood as a barrier between our world and the realm of darkness. Now, legions of daemons are attempting to climb the hill to unleash chaos upon the land. As the last guardian, it's up to you to stop them! The fate of the world rests on your shoulders. Will you rise to the challenge and defend the Sacred Hill?

How to Play

  • Place Towers: Use your starting budget to strategically place upgradable towers in the green areas to thwart the daemons.
  • Earn Points: Gain points and additional resources for each daemon you defeat.
  • Protect Your Lives: You have 100 lives. Prevent daemons from reaching the other side of the map, or you'll lose lives.
  • Adaptive Enemies: Daemons will adjust their path to avoid your towers. Plan your defenses wisely!
  • Endless Waves: Survive for as long as you can against the relentless onslaught.

Tips for Victory

  • Upgrade your towers to increase their effectiveness.
  • Balance your spending between new towers and upgrades.
  • Anticipate daemon routes and block them efficiently.
